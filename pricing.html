Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What constitutes a measurable result?

Accuracy rates, processing times, error reduction, SLA improvements, and resource hours saved. We agree the metrics during the audit so expectations are clear from day one.

What if our requirements don't fit your three products?

We'll tell you upfront. Our technology is purpose-built for specific pension challenges. If your needs fall outside our capability, we'd rather say so than waste your time.

How long until we see results?

Most engagements show measurable improvements within the first phase of deployment. We start with the highest-impact areas and expand from there.

Is there a minimum contract period?

We keep terms flexible. The partnership continues as long as the results do. If either side isn't satisfied, we have an honest conversation about it.

Does this work for large pension schemes?

It's designed for them. Larger schemes typically have more complex rules, more data, and more at stake — which means more opportunity for our technology to deliver significant improvements.
