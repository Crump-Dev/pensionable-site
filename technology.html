Our Technology

Purpose-built AI architecture that brings mathematical certainty to pension calculations, data processing, and compliance.

Intelligent Architecture

Our platform uses specialised AI components working as a coordinated system. Rather than relying on a single general-purpose tool, each component handles a specific task — interpreting scheme rules, validating data, executing calculations, and ensuring compliance.

This modular approach means every component is optimised for its role. Rule interpretation requires different capabilities than calculation execution. Data validation demands different rigour than report generation. By building purpose-specific components, we achieve the precision and auditability that pension operations demand.

The result: mathematical certainty that's traceable, repeatable, and independently verifiable at every step.

How It Works

Built for Pensions

Every component is purpose-built for pension operations. Nothing generic, nothing bolted on.

Rule Interpretation

Converts natural language scheme rules into mathematically precise executable logic. Identifies ambiguities and resolves them before they become calculation errors.

Automated Validation

Generates comprehensive test cases covering every rule variation and edge case. Validates against synthetic data — no member data required during testing.

Precision Calculation

High-performance processing with complete audit trails. Every calculation is traceable, repeatable, and independently verifiable.

Secure Data Handling

Manages data between systems with format transformation, integrity checking, and full lineage tracking throughout.
